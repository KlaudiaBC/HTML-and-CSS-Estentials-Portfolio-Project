Alpha mode

Our brain constantly produces waves of energy, which can be measured. Scientists stand out 4 types of brain activity based on those measures. Amount of waves is higher when we are awake and active and much lower during sleep.

Jose Silva based his self-help and meditation program on the Alpha Dimension.. He found out that people who can control their thoughts in Alpha mode are able to program their mind and overcome many problems, including anxiety, headache, insomnia or chronic fatigue.

Our community is focusing on training the various meditation techniques in order to master mind control in Alpha mode and overcome bad habits, trauma, gain the power of positivity and vital energy.

Breath

Breath is life. Without oxygen we die. Breathing is important not only during meditation. Controlled breathing is also beneficial for our daily life. Oxygen helps our brain work and support every system in our body.

Effective breathing can improve your sleep, help you digest food, boost your immune system or reduce stress. Check out the video below to start training your breathing techniques.

Clarity

Once you take a short time during the day to give your body and mind a rest, you will also become more and more self aware. In order to achieve peace and balance in life, you need to listen to your inner-self. This is the only way to connect with the universe and follow the simple path it has provided. Doing in life anything only to please other people is like an open battle with your fate. There is no peace in battle. It is crucial for you to understand who you are.
